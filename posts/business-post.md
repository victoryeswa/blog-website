# Kenyan Business: Avocado Boom and Economic Challenges

## Economic Overview
Kenya's economy shows resilience with rising avocado exports, but drought poses threats to key sectors.

## Highlights
- **Avocado Exports**: Projected 20% increase in 2026.
- **Milk Supply Crisis**: Output at 22-month low due to drought.
- **Clean Energy Funding**: Sh5.5bn sought for sustainable cooking solutions.

## Market Insights
Investors eye agriculture and renewable energy as growth areas. However, climate impacts require adaptive strategies.

## Outlook
With strategic investments, Kenya can leverage its agricultural strengths for sustainable development.
